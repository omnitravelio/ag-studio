Good fit
  • You have an audience, roughly 5K and up, that actually engages
  • People ask you for something you can't currently sell them
  • You know what you're good at and can explain it in a call
  • You want to own the product outright, not rent a platform
Bad fit
  • You want someone to grow the audience. I don't do that, and be careful with anyone who says they can
  • You want it in two days. Rushed products are the reason people distrust digital products
  • You're not sure what you'd even sell. Come back when you are, it'll be a better build
  • You want me to take a cut of sales instead of a fee. I build it, you keep it
