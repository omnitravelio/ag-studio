SCOPE

Problem

Product
Pricing system at $149

Freelancers think they have a pricing problem. They have a scoping problem, and the pricing problem is downstream of it. So the product had to do two things: produce a defensible number, then hold it when a client pushes.

The sales page runs a live calculator that tells a visitor exactly how much money they are losing at their current rate. Good fit

  • You have an audience, roughly 5K and up, that actually engages
  • People ask you for something you can't currently sell them
  • You know what you're good at and can explain it in a call
  • You want to own the product outright, not rent a platform

Bad fit

  • You want someone to grow the audience. I don't do that, and be careful with anyone who says they can
  • You want it in two days. Rushed products are why people distrust digital products
  • You're not sure what you'd sell. Come back when you are, it'll be a better build
  • You want me to take a cut of sales instead of a fee. I build it, you keep it
